About Me

Hello! I'm Chris, a Software Enginner based in Boston, MA. I have a passion for developing software and encountering new and exciting challenges. Through the course of my professional career and through working on personal projects, I've been designing, building, and delivering exceptional code across different technology stacks.
Currently, I am working as a full-stack web developer (although mainly back-end focused) at Bamboo Rose, a wonderful startup-like B2B software company, building a Multi-Enterprise PLM Platform.
Outside of software, I am an active individual and enjoy spending a lot of time studying and practicing various forms of martial arts. I had previously co-founded a self defense studio where I was teaching students practical street self-defense, combining styles from Boxing, Krav Maga, Muay Thai and Systema.
